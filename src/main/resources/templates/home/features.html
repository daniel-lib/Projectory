Features & Services
Projectory's core services are designed to help you and your team plan, manage, and deliver projects successfully. By using Projectory, you can improve your team's productivity, efficiency, and collaboration.
Task management:
  • Create, assign, and track tasks.
  • Set priorities and due dates.
  • Break down large tasks into smaller, more manageable tasks.
  • See how tasks are progressing in real time.
Collaboration:
  • Share tasks, files, and comments with team members.
  • Discuss tasks and projects in real time.
  • Get notified of updates to tasks and projects.
Reporting and analytics:
  • Generate reports on project progress, task completion, and resource utilization.
  • Identify bottlenecks and risks.
  • Make data-driven decisions about your projects.
Integrations:
  • Integrate with other tools that you use, such as CRM, time tracking, and accounting software.
  • Automate tasks and workflows.
  • Streamline your project management process.
Additional features:
  • Gantt charts and Kanban boards for visualizing project progress.
  • Custom fields for tracking specific information about your projects and tasks.
  • Time tracking and budgeting tools.
  • Responsive design perfect for Mobile devices.
Benefits of using Projectory:
  • Increased productivity and efficiency
  • Improved collaboration
  • Reduced risk and stress
  • Better visibility and control
  • Data-driven decision making
  • Scalability and flexibility
