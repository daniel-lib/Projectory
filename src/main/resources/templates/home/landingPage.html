Core Features.

Projectory's core services are designed to help you and your team plan, manage, and deliver projects successfully. By using Projectory, you can improve your team's productivity, efficiency, and collaboration.

Task management

Create, assign, and track tasks. See how tasks are progressing in real time.

Collaboration

Get notified of updates to tasks and projects. Share tasks, files, and comments with team members.

Kanban boards

Great way for visualizing project progress.

Reporting and analytics

Generate reports on project progress, task completion, and resource utilization.

Social experience

Meet new people. Create community & participate public projects

Data-driven decision making

Make decisions based on progress data.

Projectory can help you take the lead and be the pro in your projects.
